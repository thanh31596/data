Why Typography is Important

Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed. Good typography enhances the user experience and helps convey the tone and message of your content.

Studies show that good typography can increase reading speed, comprehension, and overall user satisfaction with a website or application.

Color in Web Design

Color is a powerful tool in web design that can:

  • Evoke emotions and set the mood
  • Guide users through an interface
  • Reinforce brand identity
  • Improve readability and accessibility

When choosing colors, consider contrast, harmony, and accessibility for all users.
