Note for HCI Students: In Option 2, users verify their email address first, then provide personal information, and finally set preferences. This design ensures:
  • Valid email addresses are collected upfront, reducing spam accounts
  • User's identity is partially verified before collecting additional personal data
  • Trade-off: Interruption in user flow by requiring them to check email during registration
  • Reduced data collection from users who abandon the process

Consider how this flow affects user experience, conversion rates, and data security compared to Option 1.
