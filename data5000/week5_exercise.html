AI-Assisted Business Analytics Exercise

TeleConnect Customer Churn Analysis

Learning Objectives

  • Collaborate effectively with Large Language Models for business analytics
  • Apply the 4-level analytics framework to real business problems
  • Transform technical insights into actionable business recommendations
  • Develop professional data science communication skills

Duration: 3 hours | Individual Exercise | LLM Collaboration Required

Business Scenario

TeleConnect - Regional Telecommunications Provider

Business Problem: 20% annual customer churn rate impacting profitability and market position

Your Role: Business Analytics Consultant

Executive Question: "Which customers will churn next, why are they leaving, and what specific actions should we take to retain them?"

Available Resources: Customer dataset, LLM collaboration tools, Python/Google Colab

Real Business Impact

Each churned customer represents approximately $1,200 in lost lifetime value. With 50 customers in our analysis sample, retention improvements could directly impact revenue and competitive positioning.

Your Dataset: customer_churn_4800.csv

📊 Download the Dataset

Access the dataset here: customer_churn_4800.csv

Dataset Structure: 50 customers × 14 features

Numerical Variables (6):

• Age: Customer age (years)

• MonthlyCharges: Monthly bill amount ($)

• TotalCharges: Lifetime spending ($)

• DataUsageGB: Monthly data consumption

• CustomerServiceCalls: Support interactions

• AccountAge: Customer tenure (months)

Categorical Variables (7):

• Gender: Male/Female

• ContractLength: Month-to-month, One year, Two year

• PaymentMethod: Electronic check, Mailed check, Bank transfer, Credit card

• InternationalPlan: Yes/No

• DeviceProtection: Yes/No

• StreamingService: Yes/No

• Churn: Yes/No (Target Variable)

Phase 1: Descriptive Analytics

Goal: Understand "What happened to our customers?"

Duration: 30 minutes | Focus: Data exploration and customer profiling

Customer Demographics Analysis

  • Age distribution and gender breakdown
  • Contract length preferences
  • Payment method popularity
  • Account age patterns

Service & Pricing Patterns

  • Monthly and total charges distribution
  • Data usage patterns by customer segment
  • Service adoption rates (International, Protection, Streaming)
  • Customer service interaction frequency
LLM Starter Prompt: "I have a telecom customer dataset with 50 customers and these columns: CustomerID, Age, Gender, MonthlyCharges, TotalCharges, ContractLength, DataUsageGB, CustomerServiceCalls, PaymentMethod, AccountAge, InternationalPlan, DeviceProtection, StreamingService, Churn. Help me create Python code to: 1. Load the data and perform initial data quality checks 2. Generate summary statistics for all numerical variables 3. Create visualizations showing customer distribution by demographics 4. Analyze service adoption patterns and pricing structures Please explain each step and provide business interpretation of the results."

Phase 2: Diagnostic Analytics

Goal: Understand "Why do TeleConnect customers churn?"

Duration: 45 minutes | Focus: Root cause analysis and correlation discovery

Churn Pattern Analysis

  • Overall churn rate calculation
  • Churn by demographic segments
  • Contract length impact on retention
  • Payment method correlation with churn

Service Impact Investigation

  • Customer service calls vs churn relationship
  • Service add-on retention effects
  • Pricing sensitivity analysis
  • Data usage patterns by churn status
LLM Advanced Prompt: "Help me perform comprehensive churn analysis on my telecom data: - Calculate churn rates by ContractLength, PaymentMethod, and demographic segments - Create correlation matrix between all numerical variables and churn - Compare average charges and service adoption between churned vs retained customers - Generate statistical significance tests for key relationships - Create visualizations that clearly show the strongest churn drivers - Explain what each finding means for TeleConnect's retention strategy Focus on actionable insights that a telecom executive could implement."

Phase 3: Predictive Analytics

Goal: Predict "Which customers will churn next?"

Duration: 60 minutes | Focus: Machine learning model development and interpretation

Model Development

  • Data preprocessing for categorical variables
  • Cross-validation strategy for small dataset
  • Multiple algorithm comparison
  • Performance evaluation and model selection

Model Interpretation

  • Feature importance analysis
  • SHAP value calculations
  • Individual customer risk scoring
  • High-risk customer profiling
LLM Model Building Prompt: "Build a customer churn prediction model for this telecom dataset: - Handle categorical variables (Gender, ContractLength, PaymentMethod, etc.) appropriately - Use stratified cross-validation since we have only 50 samples - Train and compare Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and LightGBM models - Evaluate using accuracy, precision, recall, and F1-score - Select the best model and explain why it's optimal for business use - Generate feature importance and SHAP values for interpretation - Score all customers on churn probability and identify top 10 at-risk customers Include business explanations for technical decisions and model outputs."

Phase 4: Prescriptive Analytics

Goal: Determine "What specific actions should TeleConnect take?"

Duration: 45 minutes | Focus: Causal analysis and actionable business strategy

Causal Analysis

  • Treatment effect estimation for key factors
  • Confounding variable identification
  • Service add-on causal impact assessment
  • Contract length causal effects

Strategic Recommendations

  • Segment-specific retention strategies
  • ROI calculations for interventions
  • Implementation timeline and priorities
  • Success metrics and monitoring plan
LLM Strategy Prompt: "Based on my churn analysis, help me create actionable business strategy: - Perform causal analysis to identify true drivers vs correlations - Design targeted retention interventions for different customer segments - Calculate ROI assuming average customer lifetime value of $1,200 - Prioritize recommendations by cost-effectiveness and implementation difficulty - Create 90-day action plan with specific metrics and milestones - Design A/B testing framework to validate intervention effectiveness Focus on realistic strategies that a regional telecom company could implement."

Phase 1: Descriptive Analytics Questions

Answer these questions using your descriptive analysis:

Customer Demographics

  • What is the age range and average age of TeleConnect customers?
  • What percentage of customers are male vs female?
  • What is the distribution of account ages? Are most customers new or long-term?
  • How many customers fall into each contract length category?

Service & Financial Patterns

  • What are the minimum, maximum, and average monthly charges?
  • What percentage of customers have international plans, device protection, and streaming services?
  • What is the average data usage across all customers?
  • How many customer service calls does the average customer make?

Documentation Required

Document your findings with specific numbers, percentages, and create at least 3 visualizations showing key patterns. Include business interpretation for each statistic.

Phase 2: Diagnostic Analytics Questions

Answer these questions to understand churn drivers:

Churn Rate Analysis

  • What is TeleConnect's overall churn rate?
  • Which contract length has the highest churn rate? Provide specific percentages.
  • Do customers with more customer service calls churn more? Show the correlation.
  • Which payment method has the lowest churn rate?

Service Impact Analysis

  • Do customers with international plans churn more or less than those without?
  • What is the churn rate for customers with device protection vs without?
  • How does streaming service adoption relate to customer retention?
  • Are higher monthly charges associated with lower churn rates?

Required Analysis

Create correlation matrix and provide the top 5 factors most strongly correlated with churn. Explain what each correlation means for business strategy.

Phase 3: Predictive Analytics Questions

Answer these questions using your predictive models:

Model Performance

  • Which algorithm (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, LightGBM) performed best and why?
  • What is your model's accuracy, precision, and recall?
  • What are the top 3 most important features for predicting churn?
  • How confident are you in your model's predictions given the dataset size?

Risk Assessment

  • List the CustomerIDs and churn probabilities for the top 10 highest-risk customers.
  • What characteristics do high-risk customers share?
  • If these top 10 customers churn, what is the estimated revenue loss?
  • Can you explain why your model flagged specific customers as high-risk?

Required Output: High-Risk Customer Profile

CustomerID | Churn Probability | Age | Contract | Monthly Charges | Key Risk Factors

Data Quality & Validation Questions

Ensure data integrity and analysis validity:

Data Quality Assessment

  • Are there any missing values in the dataset? How did you handle them?
  • Do all customer ages fall within reasonable ranges (18-100)?
  • Are monthly charges consistent with total charges based on account age?
  • Do any customers have negative data usage or impossible service call counts?

Analysis Validation

  • Do your findings align with general telecom industry patterns?
  • Can you explain any surprising or counterintuitive results?
  • What limitations does the small sample size (50 customers) impose on your conclusions?
  • Which insights are you most confident in and why?

Critical Thinking Check

Question your assumptions: If a correlation contradicts business logic, investigate further. Small datasets can produce misleading patterns.
