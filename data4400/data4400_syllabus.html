This subject provides students with strategies for using longitudinal data to forecast scenarios that evolve over time. Students will be introduced to the role of time-series forecasting via the formation and testing of hypotheses, application of forecasting methods, and the use of state-of-the-art software to visualise the results.


Students will learn to anticipate forecasting questions business leaders may pose to their analytics teams and develop skills in both quantitative and qualitative forecasting techniques.

Key Focus: This course emphasizes practical application of forecasting methods using real-world business scenarios and data-driven decision making.

LO4: Compare time series forecasting methods.
