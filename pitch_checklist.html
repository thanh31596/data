â–¼

0/5
ðŸ”¥ CRITICAL: Problem clearly derived from dataset patterns
The business problem must be evident in your data visualizations. This is non-negotiable for grading.
Problem quantified with specific metrics
Use numbers: "20% decrease in sales", "$2M in losses", "40% customer churn rate".
Before/after or trend visualizations prepared
Show how the problem evolved over time or compare different scenarios.
Financial impact of problem calculated
What does this problem cost? Lost revenue, increased expenses, missed opportunities?

Why does this need to be solved NOW? What happens if we don't act?
